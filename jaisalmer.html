JAISALMER

Enjoy a desert safari ??

Jaislmer is frequently visited place by tourist.

Patwon Ki haweli ??

Dipped in an enchanting shade of yellow, Patwon ki Haveli grabs the attention of every visitor. It is an impressive monument of Jaisalmer as it is the first one to have been erupted.

salim singh Haveli ??

Salim Singh ki Haveli is a beautiful edifice at the heart of the city Jaisalmer. It is one of the major tourist attractions built over the remains of an older haveli in the late 19th century.

Pokharan Fort ??

The majestic Pokhran fort has a huge door with sinister spikes jutting out to stop elephant stampede..

Sagar Lack ??

The Amar Sagar lake is an oasis near the palace of Amar Singh. It is adjacent to the 17th-century citadel which was constructed by Maharawal Akhai Singh and is located jaislmer.

Jaislmer Fort??

Jaisalmer Fort, nestled amid the golden sands of Thar Desert is steeped with rich past and heritage..

Enjoy a Boat ride at gadisar lack ??

On the outskirts of Jaisalmer in the arid state of Rajasthan lies the filled to the brim Gadisar Lake like an oasis in the heart of a desert.

View Of Vyas Chhatri ??

Vyas Chhatri is the assemblage of golden sandstone, an edifice of Rajasthani architecture.
