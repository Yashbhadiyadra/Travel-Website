* MAKE EASY TRIP Limited is an Indian online travel company founded in 2020.

* The company provides online travel services including flight tickets, domestic and Indian places holiday packages, hotel reservations, and rail and bus tickets. As of 31 january 2020.

* They have 14 company-owned travel stores in 14 cities, over 30 south indian-owned travel stores in 28 cities, and counters in four major airports in India.

* Make Easy Trip has offices in New York, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Phuket, Bangkok, and Dubai.
